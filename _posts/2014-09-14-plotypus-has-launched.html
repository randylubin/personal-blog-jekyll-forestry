I’m am very pleased to announce that I’ve launched Plotypus - the collaborative story telling card game I’ve been developing this year. See this post for an overview of the game.

Launching means that I’ve finalized the designs and created a website for it. I’m sharing the designs as a Creative Commons Licensed PDF which you can print at home (or through Print & Play Productions).

I’m really happy with how the game turned out! I’ll be doing a small batch print run for friends and family. If you’re interested, fill out this form; it’ll be $15 and I’ll mail it within the US.

Next steps are up in the air. I’m keen to hear back from players on how I can improve the game and manual. If there is interest, I’d like to Kickstart a version with professionally designs and I have a bunch of expansion packs in mind.

If you want to stay current on Plotypus news, follow this blog or subscribe to the newsletter.
