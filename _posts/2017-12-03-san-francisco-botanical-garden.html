This afternoon, Avital and I walked around the San Francisco Botanical Garden in Golden Gate Park. It’s a huge park that is broken into sections with flora from around the world. It’s also free for SF residents.


I highly recommend checking it out! I’m also tempted to write a game meant to be played in the garden.
