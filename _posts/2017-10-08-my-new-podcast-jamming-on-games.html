I’ve launched my new podcast and you can find it at JammingOnGames.com

The first episode has me workshopping my design for the Golden Cobra competition. I had on Nick Punt as a guest and we brainstormed ways to improve the design. It was super helpful and I’m happy with the final design. The game is called Among the Mortals and you can check out the pdf.

I’m hoping to record a few more episodes this month and release a new one every 2-4 weeks. Should be fun!
