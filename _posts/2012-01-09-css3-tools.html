CSS3 is awesome.  It allows you to easily make your website stylish, without having to muck about in photoshop.  Without images, the pages load faster.

Some things CSS3 allows:

  • Color Gradients
  • Shadows
  • Rounded Edges

Here are two awesome tools that generate cross-browser CSS3… use them!

http://css3generator.com/

http://www.colorzilla.com/gradient-editor/
