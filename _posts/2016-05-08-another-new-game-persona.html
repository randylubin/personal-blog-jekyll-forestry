Yesterday a playtested a new game that I’m tentatively calling Persona.

It’s a quick game that has a group collaboratively creating characters by adding on each other’s ideas. It’s inspired by the surrealist game exquisite corpse and my desire to create a game that generates novel characters. This my third game in a series that explores elements of story, the first two being Plotypus (plot) and Panorama (setting).

Check it out here and I’d love feedback!
